# GRC-Analyst-Project
You are the Junior GRC Analyst at TechNova Solutions, a fictional SaaS startup.

### Business profile

TechNova provides a web-based platform to business customers.
It stores customer data in an application database.
The company has a small team of 10–20 employees.
Its core environment includes a web app, backend services, database, cloud hosting, employee laptops, and admin accounts.

### Your mission

Build a mini compliance program that looks credible for an early-stage company and aligns with:

ISO/IEC 27001 as the ISMS structure, with Annex A used as the control reference set.
NIST CSF 2.0 as an easy-to-explain risk and governance lens.
Optional references to NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 for concrete control families when you want stronger mapping detail. NIST CSF 2.0 is organized into six Functions: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover, while SP 800-53 provides a control catalog for security and privacy controls.
Final portfolio output

By the end, you will have:

- ISMS Scope Document

- Risk Assessment (Excel)

- Risk Treatment Plan

- Security Policies (Word/PDF)

- Control Mapping (ISO 27001 Annex A or NIST)

- Audit Checklist

- Audit Report


Your role is to:
understand the business,
identify risks,
document controls,
highlight gaps,
recommend treatment actions,
and support audit readiness.

That is exactly what this project will show.
